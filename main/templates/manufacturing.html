MANUFACTURING FACILITIES

Alco Pharma has a state of the art pharmaceutical facility, a three-storied building with a foundation of seven storied and 8000 sq.ft. per floor, at Mirpur, Dhaka. Our manufacturing facilities utilize highly sophisticated machineries and equipments that enable us to produce the best in class pharmaceutical products that we are committed to our patients and caregivers.

Alco Pharma has a broad range of pharmaceutical formulations like Tablet, Capsule, Oral Liquid/ PFS, Topical Cream/Ointment/Powder.

At present our Tablet production capacity is 500 million units and capsule production capacity is 175 million units per annum. Liquid/ Dry syrup production capacity is 35 million units and Cream/Ointment production capacity is 8 million units per annum.
