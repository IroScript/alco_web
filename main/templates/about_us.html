Overview

Alco Pharma Ltd is a fastest growing pharmaceutical company in Bangladesh with nearly 2,000 dedicated employees. We at Alco work to prevent and treat the diseases we know about, and to discover and develop treatments for the ones we don't. We are committed to develop safe and effective medicines for healthy living of patients and their families.

We at Alco Pharma Ltd, always believe in Quality never ends and refuse to settle for anything until it exceeds the existing standard.

Alco's activities underscore the Company's well-defined Mission to improve the quality of care and treatment of patients by providing effective therapies, products and specialized programs.

Alco Pharma Ltd and its people are working hand-in-hand to become one of the respected and leading pharmaceutical Company in Bangladesh.
